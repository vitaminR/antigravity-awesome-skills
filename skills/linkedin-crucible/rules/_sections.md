# Rule Sections for LinkedIn Crucible

This document defines the sections for organizing LinkedIn Crucible rules.

## Prefixes and Categories

### Content Crucible (`content-`)
Rules for post creation, voice, messaging, and storytelling.

**Subcategories:**
- `content-voice-` Voice and brand consistency
- `content-story-` Storytelling and narrative structure
- `content-headline-` Headlines and hooks
- `content-visual-` Visual composition and design
- `content-cta-` Calls-to-action and conversion

**Example Prefixes:**
- `content-voice-consistency.md`
- `content-story-structure.md`
- `content-headline-optimization.md`

### Growth Crucible (`growth-`)
Rules for audience expansion, engagement, and community building.

**Subcategories:**
- `growth-audience-` Audience targeting and segmentation
- `growth-engagement-` Engagement strategies and sequences
- `growth-network-` Networking and relationship building
- `growth-amplification-` Content amplification and reach

**Example Prefixes:**
- `growth-audience-targeting.md`
- `growth-engagement-sequences.md`
- `growth-network-outreach.md`

### Operations Crucible (`ops-`)
Rules for analytics, scheduling, compliance, and measurement.

**Subcategories:**
- `ops-analytics-` Analytics and tracking
- `ops-scheduling-` Post scheduling and timing
- `ops-compliance-` Platform compliance and safety
- `ops-reporting-` Performance reporting and dashboards

**Example Prefixes:**
- `ops-analytics-tracking.md`
- `ops-scheduling-optimal-times.md`
- `ops-compliance-verification.md`

## Tagging System

All rules must include relevant tags for cross-referencing.

**Available Tags:**
- `strategy` - Strategic planning
- `execution` - Tactical execution
- `measurement` - Analytics and metrics
- `audience` - Audience-focused
- `content-creation` - Content production
- `automation` - System automation
- `brand` - Brand and voice
- `algorithm` - LinkedIn algorithm optimization
- `growth` - Audience growth
- `engagement` - Engagement tactics
- `compliance` - Platform compliance
- `timing` - Scheduling and timing
- `psychology` - Psychology and persuasion
- `video` - Video-specific
- `article` - Article-specific
- `post` - Regular post-specific
- `carousel` - Carousel-specific

## File Naming Convention

`[prefix]-[descriptor-words].md`

**Examples:**
- `content-voice-consistency.md`
- `growth-audience-targeting.md`
- `ops-analytics-setup.md`
- `content-headline-curiosity-gaps.md`
- `growth-engagement-first-hour.md`

**Naming Rules:**
1. Always use lowercase
2. Use hyphens to separate words
3. Keep filename to 50 characters or less
4. Be descriptive in the second part
